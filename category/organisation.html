IWCS 2015

Welcome

The 11th International Conference on Computational Semantics (IWCS 2015) will be held at Queen Mary University of London, UK on 14-17th April 2015. IWCS is the bi-yearly meeting of SIGSEM, the ACL special interest group on semantics.
